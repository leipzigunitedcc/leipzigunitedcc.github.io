About Us

We are a Cricket Club based out of Leipzig, Germany. Along with enjoy playing cricket, our motive is also to bring this lovely sport to wider nationalities living in Leipzig. We believe sport has the power to inspire, unite people and lets people involved, experience different cultures, create hope. We are happy to add as many interested players as possible to the club.

Our Teams

We have several sports teams that compete in local leagues and tournaments:

  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Shuttle
  • Table Tennis
